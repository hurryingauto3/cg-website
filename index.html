The Project and Previous Renders

This Ray Tracing project involved extending out previous ray tracing engine. New materials were added and ray-tracing was accelerated through acceleration structures. The project previously had the following rendered images:

Concept

Our concept is to display various polygons that are randomly distributed and colored to showcase the versitality of our Ray-Tracer

Scene

We have not particularly created a scene for our renders. Instead our focus was on generating different images to show the capabilities of our Ray-Tracer.

Code Features

We implemented the following features in our code in order to generate our Ray-Traced images

Lighting

We have added Point, Directional, and Spotlight

BRDFs

Bi-Directional Reflectance Distribution Functions (BRDFs) were used

Acceleration

To speed up the rendering of our images we used Bounding Volume Hierarchy (BVH)

Tracers

We've implmented a basic Tracer, a Relfective Tracer, and a Reflective Tracer with Shadows

Geometry

We implmented different classes for different geometries to ensure diversity in out renders

Materials

Tinged Mirror, Glossy Specular, and Transparent Materials have been implemented
